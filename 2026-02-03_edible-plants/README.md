# edible-plants
Analysis of edible plant profiles using the TidyTuesday 2026-02-03 dataset. <br/>
data source: https://github.com/rfordatascience/tidytuesday/blob/main/data/2026/2026-02-03/readme.md

## Aim
Explores the preferred soil pH ranges of edible plants grouped by cultivation type, with colour indicating median hardiness. 

## Finding
Analysis indicates that most cultivation groups have a similar optimal pH range of approximately 5–8, with no meaningful relationship observed between hardiness and pH range across groups. After aggregation, no cultivation groups have a median hardiness classified as "Very Tender," and the data suggest that extreme soil pH levels are generally unsuitable for all crop types.
